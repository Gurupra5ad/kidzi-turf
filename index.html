Who are we ?

Kidzi Turf - Multi sports engagement for kidz

At Kidzi Turf we believe physical literacy is one simple solution to help kids become more active, healthy, and successful for life. It also aids in improving academic performance, cognitive skills, mental health, psychological wellness, social skills, and healthy lifestyle habits.

Our Multi-Sports Program provides the direction, education and inspire kids through practical & engaging training to attain physical literacy. We are enablers of life-long learning and strongly believe in starting at an early age.
